South Korea

Cost-based pool · ~121 GW installed capacity

South Korea operates a cost-based pool market through KPX (Korea Power Exchange). KEPCO is the dominant integrated utility controlling transmission and distribution. The government is expanding nuclear capacity while rolling out offshore wind, particularly in the Yellow Sea.
